# Executive Summary

Every successful enterprise project depends on understanding the people who influence, use, support, and benefit from the solution.

This stakeholder analysis identifies the individuals, business functions, and technical teams involved in SmartChain Nexus™ and defines their roles, interests, expectations, influence, and communication needs throughout the project lifecycle.

---

# Stakeholder Objectives

The objectives of this document are to:

- Identify all project stakeholders.
- Understand stakeholder expectations.
- Define communication strategies.
- Support effective decision-making.
- Reduce project risks.
- Improve collaboration.

# Primary Stakeholders

## Project Sponsor

**Role**

Project Owner

**Responsibilities**

- Define project vision
- Approve deliverables
- Prioritize objectives
- Review progress

---

## Supply Chain Manager

Responsible for improving operational efficiency across procurement, warehousing, logistics, and inventory management.

### Expectations

- Better visibility
- Accurate KPIs
- Inventory optimization
- Demand forecasting

---

## Procurement Manager

Responsible for supplier performance and purchasing.

### Needs

- Supplier analytics
- Lead-time visibility
- Spend analysis
- Purchase trends

---

## Warehouse Manager

Responsible for warehouse operations.

### Needs

- Capacity utilization
- Inventory movement
- Picking efficiency
- Storage optimization

---

## Logistics Manager

Responsible for transportation and delivery performance.

### Needs

- Delivery analytics
- Route performance
- Transportation KPIs
- Shipment tracking

---

## Executive Leadership

Uses strategic dashboards.

### Needs

- Executive KPIs
- Forecasts
- Business trends
- Operational risks

---

## Data Analyst

### Responsibilities

- Validate datasets
- Generate reports
- Support business decisions

## Data Scientist

Responsible for predictive analytics.

- Evaluate performance

## Software Development Team
